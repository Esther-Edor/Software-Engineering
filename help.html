frequently asked questions

  • How do I search for products?

Click on the category name on the topbar of the homepage to view the products we have within those categories. You can also use the search bar located at the top of our homepage to find your preferred products.

  • How do I place an order on your website?

Once you have found your preferred product, just follow the steps below:

- Select your preferred colour/size and then click on ‘buy this now’, to add this product into your cart.

- Click on the ‘proceed to checkout’ tab to complete your order

- At checkout, you will need to fill in your personal details and shipping address if you are a new customer.

- If you are a returning customer, please log-in

If you encounter any problems while placing your order, do not hesitate to contact us at +233 57 880 7209 or email us at customer-service@antaanra.com

  • How do I register?

Kindly click on the register tab located at the top right end of our homepage.

A screen will pop up asking you to log-in if you are an existing customer or to continue if you are a new customer.

Once you click on continue, you will be required to fill in some personal details.

After updating the required fields, click on the submit tab to create your account.

  • How can I sell my products on your website?

To become a business partner, kindly send an email with your information on your product and company information to marketplace@antaanra.com.gh.

Please include photos and information about your products. A member of our vendor management team will contact you shortly.

  • When will I get my product after I place an order (delivery period)?

Our standard delivery timeline is 3-10 business days. As soon as your order is dispatched from our warehouse, you will receive an email to confirm that your package is on its way. You will be contacted by telephone by our dispatch personnel on the day of delivery to confirm your availability.

  • Can I pick up my item instead of having it delivered?

Antaanra.com offers a convenient delivery service to your home or office. We are unable to give customer packages from our warehouse.

  • My items haven't arrived yet. What can I do?

If you have not received your items or heard from our dispatch team 5 days after placing your order, kindly contact us on +233 57 880 7209 or send an email as customer-service@antaanra.com

- Make sure you have your order number handy. Your order number will be in the e-mail you received when your order was confirmed.

We strive to always offer our customer the best quality products at a competitive price. We do not negotiate price.
